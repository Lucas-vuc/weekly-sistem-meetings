Reports every Monday. Owner accountable for weekly progress.

Pipeline rules

No Jira ticket
The idea does not exist. Ticket created before the Ideas Session closes — no exceptions.
No owner
Cannot pass Under Review. Owner = whoever proposed it or actively champions it.
MVP exists
Skip Idea Pool. Enter Incubation directly. Still requires a Jira ticket and a named owner on day one.
Fast-track
Any founder can escalate to Approved — but must create ticket + owner + Jira subtasks in the same act.
Urgency unwritten
Urgency stated verbally does not count. It must be written in the Jira ticket. Unwritten urgency is not urgency.
Killed / Parked
Reason documented in Jira. Never deleted. Parked ideas reviewed every 90 days in Strategic Review.
When does it enter the Weekly Execution?
Only if its Jira status changed this week — new, escalated, or closed. No change = not discussed. The section is skipped entirely if nothing changed.
What happens when it enters?
Owner presents in 60 seconds: what changed, what is the action. Action becomes a child Jira ticket linked to the R&O ticket.

Operating rules

No agenda = no meetingEvery meeting requires an agenda posted in Teams 24 hours before. No agenda means it is cancelled or rescheduled.
Execution is protectedMonday Execution is for active projects only. Zero new ideas. Zero VWC topics. This separation is non-negotiable.
VWC has its own laneVWC objectives are discussed Tuesday morning only. Mining operations never enter VUC pipeline discussions.
Decisions in the roomOperational decisions made by the project owner. Strategic decisions only in Pipeline Review, Incubation Review, or Strategic Review. Never in Teams chat.
R&O: no change = no discussionRisks and opportunities only enter the Monday agenda if their Jira status changed that week. Silence means stable.
Matrix updated monthlyThe Project Evaluation Matrix is updated once a month in Strategic Review. Scores are not changed ad hoc.
Founders as bottleneckIf a task requires founder approval, it gets a Jira ticket with a deadline. Not chased over Teams chat.
MVP fast-trackProjects with a working MVP skip Idea Pool and enter Incubation directly. Still require a Jira ticket and a named owner on day one.
Urgency must be writtenUrgency stated verbally does not move a ticket. It must be written in Jira. Unwritten urgency is not urgency.
Core principle: execution is protected from the noise of ideation. Ideas have their moment (Friday) and their place (Jira). Active projects have their moment (Monday) and their owner. VWC has its own lane (Tuesday morning). Mixing any of these three destroys team focus.
