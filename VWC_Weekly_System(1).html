Meeting structure — 30 minutes total

1
Four Pillars — Objectives Review
Each owner: this week / this month / 90-day sprint. Blockers surface immediately. No preparation needed beyond updating Jira.
15 min
2
This Week’s Objectives — set & confirm
Each objective gets a named owner and a definition of done. Logged in Jira before the meeting ends.
5 min
3
Previous Week Review
Committed vs delivered — specific and honest. Delivered / Not delivered / Partial and why.
5 min
4
Risks & Opportunities — status changes only
Only R&O tickets whose Jira status changed this week are discussed. New, escalated, or closed. If nothing changed — this section is skipped entirely.
5 min
5
Action Items & Blockers
All actions logged in Jira before the meeting closes. Owner + deadline required. No exceptions.
5 min
Ground rule: If it is not in Jira, it does not exist. Every objective, risk, opportunity, and action created in this meeting gets a Jira ticket before anyone leaves the room.
